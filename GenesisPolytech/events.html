Our Events

Welcome Event

This event will provide an opportunity to connect with other students and learn about the resources and supports available at Genesis Polytech. A panel of current students will share their journey and experiences

Youth Ball

Get ready for a fun-filled night of dance, music, all you can eat food and drinks. Indulge in fun activites and have some time away from studies. Just forget about assignments for a while and enjoy to your hearts content.

Learn n Grow

This event will provide an opportunity to connect with seniors and students from other campsus. They can learn about their experience and journey at Genesis Polytech. There will also be different panels available for different courses.

Playdate

This event will provide an opportunity for the parents to spend a whole day full of games, food and fun activites with their children. Strengthen the parent-child bond and give yourself a chance to spend a day without worrying about anything.

Women in Industry

WIT helps women gain well-paying full-time careers as well as getting managerial positions in the trades. It aims to build self-confidence and provide students with a head-start on achieving apprenticeship certification, if applicable.

Leaders Summit

This event will provide an opportunity to connect with industry experts and students will be available to learn crucial aspects of getting success at work. A panel of some famous CEOs will share their journey and experiences
